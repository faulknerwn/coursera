Want to make a skydive?

Tandem Skydiving

Tandem Skydiving is where you are strapped to an experienced instructor and come down under one large parachute. It is the safest way to make a first skydive.

Static Line

Static line is where your parachute is attached by a line to the aircraft, and it automatically opens your parachute when you leave the plane. It is most commonly seen in military operations these days.

Accelerated Freefall

This is the way most skydivers learn to do it on your own. You will have an instructor or two with you in freefall and you will learn to fly your own parachute down.
